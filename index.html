About Us

Our purpose at Above Ability Health Services is to give the finest care to our clients and to maintain the highest possible standards of integrity in each aspect of our service, thus meriting the goodwill of our clients by providing care that is specific to the needs of the clients.

Our Mission

The mission of our agency is to foster independence and to maintain the dignity of our clients. We work with our clients and their families to develop a customized plan and provide excellent home care services. Our goal is to respect the independence of our clients by helping them stay in their homes

Our Vision

To become the nation's leading provider of specialized healthcare services, setting new standards for accessibility, innovation, and patient-centered care in the disability community.

Why Choose Us

Compassionate Expertise

At Above Ability Health Services, we believe everyone deserves to age with dignity, independence, and compassion. Our non-medical home care is designed to do just that. We offer:

• Personalized Care Plans – Tailored to meet your unique needs, preferences, and routines.
• Trained, Trusted Caregivers – Carefully screened professionals who provide respectful, reliable assistance.
• Comfort of Home – We help clients remain safely in the comfort of their own homes, surrounded by familiarity and family.
• Flexible Support – Whether a few hours a week or around-the-clock care, we’re here when you need us.
• Family-Focused Service – Open communication and a true partnership with families and loved ones.

We’re more than just caregivers—we’re a caring hand, a watchful eye, and a source of peace of mind.
